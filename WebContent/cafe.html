Cafeteria

Vignan's Deemed to be University has a spacious canteen with latest equipment and hygienic environment which provides quality food and prompts service and caters to needs of all the students and the staff. Central cafeteria named The Most Happening Place is available in the campus with varieties of food. Mini cafes and fast-food centers are available in various blocks of the university. The canteen is open from 9a.m. to 5p.m. There is a wide variety of North-Indian and South-Indian cuisine and the students enjoy the pleasure of eating during the breaks. Cool aqua water for drinking is available.

There is a movable food store within the campus named The Food Truck that offers different varieties of eatables.The Canteen is open to cater to the needs of students and staff at all hours.Set in a serene, green atmosphere this canteen did not fail to attract the customers.All chicken item are sold best in our place.The canteen can accommodate at least 120 students. For lunch they offer all kinds of variety rice, Chappathi, Paratha, etc. Serving at least 150 customers a day, they do sell chocolates, biscuits, cool drinks and snacks, too.

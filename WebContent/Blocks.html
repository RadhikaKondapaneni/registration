U-block

This block is U-shaped and is also known as Aryabhatta block.It has four floors with a beautiful collection of flowering plants. There is a canteen in the ground floor along with a spacious open air theatre.It contains number of laboratories and gallery halls that are well furnished and centralised.There is a sports room in its left wing.Roof of the block is covered with solar panels that provide electricity to the campus when needed. There is a two way entrance to the above floors.It consists of various departments like:

  1. Applied Engineering
  2. Information Technology
  3. Bio Technology
  4. Mechanical Engineering
  5. Civil Engineering
  6. Bachelor of computer application
  7. Management Studies

A-block

This block is A-shaped and is also known as Administrative block.It has five floors centred with a small tree.This is the newest of all blocks and has all facilities up to date.It contains two seminar halls that can accomodate large number of students for a gathering . There is a four way entrance to the top floors.There is a canteen in the middle floor.This block is closer to the main gate and is opposite to H-block.This block also contains solar cells that generate electricity.There is a Indian flag on the top of this building.This block contains various departments like:

  1. Science and Humanities
  2. Finance Office
  3. Training and Placement Cell
  4. Exam Cell
  5. Administration Office
  6. Dean and Student Affairs

H-block

This block is H-shaped and is known as Visweswaraya block.It has three floors centred with a V-tree.This block contains two seminar halls with air conditioned capable of accomodating massive number of students.There is a two way entrance to the top floor.This block consists is neighboured with a main canteen along with a small canteen in its ground floor.It allows faculty to park their vehicles with good amount of space.This block is close to the Vignan's boys hostel and is a good choice for offering delicious food.There is a photocopy availability in this block and it is the place where faculty members are offered with free food.This block consists of various departments like:

  1. Electronics and Communication Engineering
  2. Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  3. Chemical Engineering
  4. Textile Engineering
  5. Foodtechnology
  6. Bioinformatics
  7. Biomedical

Pharmacy-block

This block is also known as J.C block and consists of pharmacy students along with Computer Science engineering.This is attractive with its flowers and greenery.We find a group of monkeys wandering within the block all the time.This is a three floored building with two floors belonging to enginnering students.There is a large playground infront of this block with its courts of badminton,volleyball,throwball etc.There is a three way entrance to this building and is always supplied with refreshing water.There is a canteen in second floor offering varities of food during college off time.This block consists of computer labs that are well furnished and are maintained with optimum temperature through air conditioning.Every floor of this building offers a wireless internet connection to students that can be usde for academic research.The top floor contains various laboratories that research scholars work on their projects.It consists of various departments like:

  1. Computer Science Engineering
  2. Pharmacy
